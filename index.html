Gods Plan For Man

Rev. Rose M. Aluli

"God has a plan to bring man back on the same level as Christ." Rose M. Aluli

This message was given To the Church on September 23, 1979.

Rose sings "Rock of Ages."

For the great plan of salvation, Lord, thank you. Thank you, Lord, for the redemption of our body, soul, and spirit, Lord, for the great plan of salvation, the redemption of the world, Lord. Through it all, the Lord has a plan that he is fulfilling. That is what we are going to talk about – man and what God's plan is for man, and how far we have missed the mark, the world in general.

It will be some things different from what perhaps some of us understand. Just hang on. If you are sincere with God and honest with God, it will be revealed to you in his time, Amen.

That was a different thing again. Here is that complete likeness of God. So there is a difference. Now I can understand why he said 'greater works than these shall ye do for I go to my Father'.

We have not seen this yet, but it is coming, Amen, because the Word promised it. We cannot just take a portion of this. We have to take every bit of it, every bit of the Word. We have to take the whole thing. Oh, we do not have to. I mean we can go on blindly, but there is no limit to God. There is no end to God. He is forever and forever and forever. And there are things that we are just scraping the surface of, that is all – of the knowledge of God.

This is where Jesus Christ came and died and shed his blood for our salvation: not just to save, although that is his greatest concern – the salvation of souls, but that he might produce a people that would be so dedicated to God and so completely in the will of God, that God could transform them. I believe every word in this book, every one of them, Amen, because I have tried it and tried it and tried it. I have proven that the Word is truth. Amen.
